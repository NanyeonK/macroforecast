FRED-MD

  • Parent: FRED datasets

  • Current dataset: FRED-MD

FRED-MD is the monthly national macro panel used by dataset=fred_md. macroforecast downloads the official current CSV from:

https://www.stlouisfed.org/-/media/project/frbstl/stlouisfed/research/fred-md/monthly/current.csv

Generated: 2026-04-30. Current data through: 2025-09. Current column count excluding the date index: 126.

Column Contract

  • Date column: sasdate, parsed as the monthly date index.

  • Data columns: one column per FRED-MD mnemonic in the official current CSV.

  • Transform row: Transform: gives the official FRED-MD T-code for each data column.

  • Description source: official FRED-MD appendix when the mnemonic is present; otherwise the FRED series page or the official appendix/change log.
