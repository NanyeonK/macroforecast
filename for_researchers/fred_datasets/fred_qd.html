FRED-QD

  • Parent: FRED datasets

  • Current dataset: FRED-QD

FRED-QD is the quarterly national macro panel used by dataset=fred_qd. macroforecast downloads the official current CSV from:

https://www.stlouisfed.org/-/media/project/frbstl/stlouisfed/research/fred-md/quarterly/current.csv

Generated: 2026-04-30. Current data through: 2025-09. Current column count excluding the date index: 245.

Column Contract

  • Date column: sasdate, parsed as the quarterly date index.

  • Data columns: one column per FRED-QD mnemonic in the official current CSV.

  • factors row: 1 means the series is in the Stock-Watson-style factor construction set; 0 means it is not.

  • Transform row: transform gives the official FRED-QD T-code for each data column.

  • Description source: official FRED-QD appendix when the mnemonic is present; otherwise the FRED series page or the official appendix/change log.
